CAPT. MANOJ KUMAR PANDEY, PVC

1/11 Gorkha Rifles

PARAM VIR CHAKRA (Posthumous)

Date of Birth: 25 June 1975

Known As: Hero of Khalubar

Battle: Operation Vijay (Kargil War), 1999
"Some goals are so worthy, it's glorious even to fail."

The Legend's Life

During his SSB interview, when asked why he wanted to join the army, Manoj Pandey replied: "I want to win the Param Vir Chakra." He didn't just join the army; he joined the 1/11 Gorkha Rifles and became a legend of the Kargil War.

Battle of Khalubar

Leading his men to capture the Khalubar Hills, he faced heavy enemy fire. Despite being wounded in the shoulder and leg, he pushed forward to destroy four enemy bunkers. He was hit by bullets in the forehead, but his last words to his men were, "Na Chodnu!" (Don't leave them).

War Cry


"JAI MAHA KALI, AAYO GORKHALI!"

"If death strikes before I prove my blood, I swear I'll kill Death!"
