CAPTAIN VIKRAM BATRA, PVC

13 JAK Rifles

PARAM VIR CHAKRA (Posthumous)

Date of Birth: 9 September 1974

Code Name: Sher Shah

Battle: Operation Vijay (Kargil War), 1999

Awards & Honours

  • Param Vir Chakra: Awarded for his bravery in capturing Point 5140 and Point 4875.

The Legend's Journey

Hailing from Palampur, Himachal Pradesh, Vikram Batra was always a leader. Known for his infectious smile and fearlessness, he was commissioned into the 13th Battalion, Jammu and Kashmir Rifles.

Peak 5140 and 4875

After capturing Point 5140, he sent the famous signal "Yeh Dil Maange More!". During the battle for Point 4875, he went to rescue a fellow officer, saying "Tu baal-bachon wala hai, hat ja peeche" (You have a family, step back), and moved forward himself.

"YEH DIL MAANGE MORE!"

— The iconic success signal from the peaks of Kargil.

"Either I will come back after hoisting the Tricolour, or I will come back wrapped in it, but I will be back for sure."

His victory cry "Durge Mata Ki Jai" still echoes in the mountains of Kargil.
